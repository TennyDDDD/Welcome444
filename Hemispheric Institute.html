Bridging Borders through Art, Scholarship, and Activism

Unveiling the Hemispheric Institute

Content Curator

The Hemispheric Institute connects artists, scholars, and activists across the Americas, emphasizing social justice through research and politically engaged performances. Interviews with event and publication directors delve into the institute's networking strategies, illustrating how live events and digital publications extend its reach beyond geographical constraints.

Established as a platform for collaboration and activism, the Hemispheric Institute has been instrumental in connecting individuals across the Americas. With a keen focus on social justice issues, the institute delves into the realm of politically engaged performance, leveraging gatherings, courses, publications, and archives to amplify its impact.

Through interviews with key figures like event and publication directors, we gain insight into the institute's multifaceted approach, which transcends geographical barriers. This interconnectedness between live events and digital publications serves to disseminate knowledge and foster networking opportunities, illustrating the institute's commitment to inclusivity and outreach.

VR Spatial Design

In this hybrid platform, South American artists find a platform for expression blending offline performances on physical stages with online database uploads, forming a robust and influential artistic community. Through dialogues, lectures, roundtable discussions, panels, artist talks, and performances, artists freely express their beliefs and viewpoints.

Our VR prototype reflects these diverse forms of artistic expression. Upon donning VR glasses, users are transported into a world of staggered circular stages, each offering unique interactive experiences and sounds corresponding to the artists' performance styles. For instance, the performance stage hosts various activities, immersing players in the lively ambiance upon entry, while floating walls symbolize the upload process to the database.

In this space, voices blend and influence one another, mirroring the platform's role in fostering a flourishing culture and tighter connections among artists.

Future design iterations will further explore the relationship between data storage and spatial experience, seamlessly integrating database functions with the immersive spatial experience.

(Researched, interviewed and designed by Xie, Xinyu and Deng, Ning)
